What you will learn in three hours

Everything below is taught with concrete examples and live walk-throughs. No prerequisites beyond curiosity.

Part 1: What AI actually is

Foundations

What is a model. What is training. What is inference. The difference between traditional software and machine learning. Where AI works well, and where it fails.

Landscape

The three families: classical machine learning, deep learning, and large language models. What each is for, and how they relate. How we got from spam filters to ChatGPT.

Part 2: How AI is built and run today

Build

Data, features, training, evaluation, deployment. The lifecycle of a real model, from raw data to production. Why teams that skip evaluation are the ones whose models break.

Run

Generative AI, agents, retrieval-augmented generation, and the toolchain that makes them work. What prompting is, what it is not, and where the real engineering lives.

Part 3: Using AI in your own work

Practitioner

How to identify problems that AI can solve, and the more important set of problems AI cannot solve. How to scope a small AI project from idea to a working demo.

Career

Which roles in AI are real, which are noise, and what background each one actually requires. How to learn AI without spending five years on a PhD.

Format

Length

Three hours, live, with a short break in the middle. Questions throughout, not just at the end.

Where

Online. In-person sessions in select Indian cities will be added based on registration density.

Replay

Price

Designed for accessibility: ₹150 to ₹400 (approximately two to five US dollars) per participant. The goal is reach, not margin.
